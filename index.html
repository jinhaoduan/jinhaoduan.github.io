Jinhao Duan (段金昊)

I am a PhD student at Drexel University, advised by Prof. Kaidi Xu.

I am interested in Trustworthy Machine Learning, including Adversarial Robustness, Uncertainty Quantification of Large Foundation Models, Security & Privacy of Generative AI, with the potential applications in healthcare.
